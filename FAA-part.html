Your First Step Toward Commercial Drone Operations

If you want to earn money with a drone, your first requirement is passing the FAA Part 107 exam for most commercial drone operations in the United States. If you want to earn money with a drone, offer services, or operate professionally, this certification is the starting point.

Key Points:
  • Required for most paid drone work
  • Helps you operate legally and professionally
  • Opens opportunities in multiple industries
  • Builds the foundation for a drone business
