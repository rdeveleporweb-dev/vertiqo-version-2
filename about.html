Why We Built Vertiqo Group

The drone industry offers incredible opportunities, yet many aspiring entrepreneurs struggle with two challenges:

  • First, earning their FAA Part 107 certification.
  • Second, figuring out how to transform that certification into a real business.

Too often, people either become certified without knowing how to find clients, or they have entrepreneurial ambitions but don't know where to begin. Vertiqo Group was created to bridge that gap.

We believe education should do more than help you pass an exam—it should provide the knowledge, confidence, and systems necessary to build something meaningful.

Our Mission

To train FAA-certified drone pilots and empower them to launch businesses with confidence through accessible, interactive, and founder-focused education.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform where aviation education and entrepreneurship come together to help drone pilots build successful businesses and create new opportunities.
